The automatic boost rewards actually grant now, and reward names follow your language.

- **Fixed: the level 15 / 50 / 100 boost rewards never granted.** The reward command passed its arguments in a form Hytale's parser silently drops, so claiming the milestone delivered nothing. The rewards now use the named-argument form and deliver their Taming XP boosts (including the all-skill boost at the cap).
- **Reward names no longer pin to English.** Reward line-items drop their baked-in English names; the plugin auto-renders each line ("Taming XP x2 for 45m") in the player's language.
